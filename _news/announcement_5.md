Excited to have started working on Neuromorphic Wearable systems at the <a href="https://lass.cs.umass.edu/">Laboratory for Advanced System Software (LASS)</a> with <a href="https://people.cs.umass.edu/~shenoy/">Prof. Prashant Shenoy</a> and <a href="https://people.cs.umass.edu/~phuc/">Prof. VP Nguyen</a>.

My research focuses on building energy-efficient, always-on wearable systems by combining neuromorphic event-driven sensing with on-device LLM inference for low-power IoT devices.
